How to Use Poker Wizzard

Adding a Card

To add a card to your hand or the community cards, follow these steps:

  • Click the "Add Card" button to start the process of adding a card.
  • Select the suit and rank of the card that you want to add from the two dropdown menu. (*Note: Cards that already exists in the player hand or community cards can not be added)
  • Press the "Confirm" button. The selected card will automatically appear on the screen to the right side of existing cards.

Deleting a Card

To delete a card from your hand or the community cards, follow these steps::

  • Tap on the card you want to remove.
  • Press the "Delete" button from the drop down to delete the selected card.
  • The card will be removed from the selected position.

Cancel Modifying a Card

If you wish to cancel the action of modifying a card:

  • Simply press the "Cancel" button on the dropdown menu.
